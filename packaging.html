My Love-ly Friend

This brand, which has no name, shift the experience from heartbreak to humorous self-care through playful packaging, and cringe slogans The brand acknowledges the common experience of being friend-zoned without judgment, it encourages social sharing with humorous captions, fostering community. In addition, it stands out in the field of generic gag gifts and even Valentine gifts by addressing a specific experience in a lighthearted manner.

My name is Hoveek and I am a brand design enthusiast with a penchant for exploration and a passion for creativity. At 21 years old, I find myself at the crossroads of cultures, blending the rich tapestries of my Armenian heritage with the vibrant energy of my Iraqi roots, all against the backdrop of the diverse Canadian landscape. My journey in design is a reflection of my eclectic interests and multicultural upbringing. From the bustling streets of Baghdad to the serene landscapes of Yerevan, each experience has shaped my unique perspective and infused my work with a depth of cultural richness.

The Wrecks

The Wrecks' Infinitely Ordinary is an indie/pop rock album with happy-sounding instrumentals throughout the songs Although the lyrics of the album are not all positive or in some cases not very serious, the loud instrumental sound dominates it and makes it sound more energetic than anything else My goal was to try to capture the feeling of the listener in the album design, and to give a similar sense of listening to the actual songs

Medium: Paper

Software: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Indesign, Adobe Photoshop

Morag Myerscough Poster Series

Morag's style of design can be described in three words, adventurous, charming, and timeless, and my goal was to capture that same feeling. My overall concept was to simplify her style while still maintaining the same look and feel by using different kinds of design principles. A combination of geometric shapes and bright color instantly screams her name, so I decided to focus on that.

Medium: Paper

Software: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Indesign
